Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Pixo Canvo ("the service") is operated as a personal / community project. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is handled. By using the service you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information we collect

Account data

When you register you provide a username and password. Passwords are stored as cryptographic hashes — the plaintext is never retained. You may optionally upload an avatar image.

Pixel activity

Every pixel placement is stored as a UserAction record containing your user identifier, the pixel coordinates, the chosen color, and a timestamp. Accepted placements are also persisted as Pixel records and used to compute leaderboard rankings.

Chat messages

Messages you send in the global canvas or inside a community are stored in the database linked to your account. Chat is publicly visible to all members of the relevant canvas or community.

IP addresses

Your IP address is logged alongside each pixel placement for rate-limiting and abuse-prevention purposes. It is not displayed to other users.

Session cookies

We use a standard Django session cookie to keep you logged in. No third-party tracking cookies are set.

2. How we use your data

  • To operate the service — authenticate you, process pixel placements, display the canvas and leaderboard.
  • To enforce the 60-second cooldown and prevent abusive placement patterns.
  • To display your username and avatar to other players within communities you belong to.
  • To calculate and display per-user pixel counts on the leaderboard and community detail pages.

3. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Data may be disclosed only if required by law or to protect the security of the service. The service relies on self-hosted or provider-managed infrastructure (Docker, PostgreSQL, Redis, Kafka); service operators may access logs for debugging and maintenance.

4. Data retention

  • • Account data — retained as long as your account exists. You may request deletion by contacting the service administrator.
  • • UserAction records — retained indefinitely for audit and leaderboard history.
  • • Chat messages — retained indefinitely; deleted if the associated community is deleted.
  • • Avatar images — deleted from storage when you replace or remove them.

5. Your rights

You can update your username, password, and avatar at any time via the Profile Settings page. To request full account deletion or a copy of your data please contact the site administrator directly. Because this is a community / hobby project there is no formal data-protection officer; requests are handled on a best-effort basis.

6. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the service after a change constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
